About this map

▲

The NYC Art Gallery Tracker started as a gallery locator. I went to a show in a gallery that, despite having walked past the building for decades, I never realized was a gallery — very low profile. That gave me the idea to map out the locations of all the galleries in NYC. You might not know there is a gallery on the very block where you are standing.

Next came the idea to mark with a green dot any gallery that has changed its show in the past 8 days. After 8 days the dot reverts to gray. With nearly 300 galleries across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island, this saves you from checking each gallery's website individually.

Use the borough buttons at the top left to filter by neighborhood.
